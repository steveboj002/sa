Frequently Asked Questions

What is StockWise Analytics?

StockWise Analytics is a service designed to provide individual investors with real-time stock analysis, customizable alerts, continuous monitoring, and historical data insights to help make informed investment decisions.

How do I get started?

Simply register for an account, log in, and start adding stock symbols to your watchlist. You can then configure your analysis preferences and alert settings.

What data providers do you use?

We currently support Alpha Vantage and Yahoo Finance (yfinance) for stock data. You can select your preferred provider on the analysis page.

Are the alerts real-time?

Our continuous analysis feature checks your watchlist at a user-defined interval (in minutes), and email alerts are sent shortly after a trigger condition is met.

Is StockWise Analytics free to use?

Currently, StockWise Analytics is in a development phase. Information on pricing models or premium features will be provided in the future.

If you have any other questions, please visit our Contact Us page.
