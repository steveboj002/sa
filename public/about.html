About StockWise Analytics

Welcome to StockWise Analytics, your intelligent partner in navigating the dynamic world of stock markets. Our mission is to empower individual investors and traders with timely, data-driven insights, making complex financial analysis accessible and actionable.

Founded on the principle that informed decisions lead to better outcomes, StockWise Analytics provides a comprehensive suite of tools designed to monitor market movements, identify potential opportunities, and alert you to critical changes in your watchlist. We leverage advanced data providers to bring you up-to-the-minute stock quotes, historical data, and sophisticated technical indicators.

Our Features Include:

  • **Real-time Stock Analysis:** Get instant insights into key metrics for your selected stocks.
  • **Customizable Alerting System:** Set personalized tolerances for price changes, volume comparisons, and moving average deviations to receive timely email notifications.
  • **Continuous Monitoring:** Keep a watchful eye on your portfolio with automated, interval-based analysis.
  • **MA200 Crossover Detection:** Be alerted to significant trend reversals with historical 200-day Moving Average crossover signals.
  • **User Watchlists:** Easily manage and analyze your favorite stocks in one place.

At StockWise Analytics, we believe in transparency, accuracy, and putting the power of financial data directly into your hands.
